ADHD-resistant Sticky Notes

An ADHD-resistant sticky note that changes color and font every 10 minutes, so your brain doesn't erase it from existence.

Features

  • A todo list looks different every 10 minutes
  • Remembers what you wrote
  • Wake up to a different-looking todo list every day
